Pizza

Description

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flattened base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients such as anchovies, mushrooms, onions, olives, pineapple, meat, etc. which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven. It is usually served hot.

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • White sugar
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
