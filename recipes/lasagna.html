Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a baked casserole made with wide flat pasta and layered with fillings such as ragú, béchamel, vegetables and various cheeses1. In other regions, lasagna can be made with various combinations of ricotta or mozzarella cheese, tomato sauce, meats (such as ground beef, pork or chicken), and vegetables (such as spinach, zucchini, olives, mushrooms), and the dish is typically flavored with wine, garlic, onion, and oregano

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Olive oil
  • Ground beef
  • Bulk Italian sausage
  • Sliced mushrooms
  • Garlic Salt
  • Dried oregano
  • Dried thyme
  • Dried basil
  • Tomato sauce
  • Salt and pepper
